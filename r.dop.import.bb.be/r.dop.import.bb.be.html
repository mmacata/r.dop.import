DESCRIPTION

r.dop.import.bb.be downloads and imports digital orthophotos (DOP) for Brandenburg and area of interest. The data "Digitale Orthophotos" is downloaded from Geoportal Brandenburg and can be used under the specification of the license: Â© GeoBasis-DE/LGB, Datenlizenz Deutschland Namensnennung 2.0, DOP.

Import DOPs

r.dop.import.bb aoi=aoi_BB output=dop_BB -r
