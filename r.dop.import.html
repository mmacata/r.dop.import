NAME

r.dop.import - Toolset for the import of digital orthophotos (DOPs). It includes import addons for the open geodata digital orthophotos for Germany.

KEYWORDS

raster, import

DESCRIPTION

The r.dop.import toolset downloads digital orthophotos for a specified aoi and multiple federal states. Overview of the available digital orthophotos (at least 20 cm spatial resolution):
Federal state DOP
Baden-Württemberg (BW) Not available
Bayern (BY) Not available
Berlin (BE) r.dop.import fs=BE
Brandenburg (BB) r.dop.import fs=BB
Bremen (HB) Not available
Hamburg (HH) Not available
Hessen (HE) TODO
Mecklenburg-Vorpommern (MV) Not available
Niedersachsen (NI) TODO
Nordrhein-Westfalen (NW) r.dop.import fs=NW
Rheinland-Pfalz (RP) Not available
Saarland (SL) Not available
Sachsen (SN) r.dop.import fs=SN
Sachsen-Anhalt (ST) Not available
Schleswig-Holstein (SH) Not available
Thüringen (TH) r.dop.import fs=TH

REQUIREMENTS

  • grass-gis-helpers>=1.1.0
